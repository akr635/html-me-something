Adventures in South Africa

May, June & July 2010

In the summer of 2010, I studied abroad in South Africa. I spent the majority of my summer in Cape Town.

Getting to South Africa was an experience in and of itself. First, some friends and I took a Greyhound bus from St. Louis to Chicago. In Chicago, we boarded a flight to Paris. We spent 10+ hours exploring Paris during our layover. Finally, we flew to Cape Town, South Africa.

While in Cape Town, I studied comparative law alongside South African law students. We focused on constitional law, criminal justice, and dispute resolution.

I had many remarkable experiences while in South Africa. Most notably:

  • Cage diving with great white sharks
  • Visiting Robben Island, where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned
  • Week-long safari in Kruger National Park
